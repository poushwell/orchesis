# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Who We Are

Orchesis is an open-source project maintained by Pavel (github.com/poushwell). This privacy policy applies to the website orchesis.io.

## What We Collect

### Website (orchesis.io)

Currently, orchesis.io does **not** collect any personal data:

- No cookies (no cookie banner needed)
- No analytics or tracking scripts
- No user accounts or registration
- No contact forms that store data
- No third-party scripts that track visitors

The website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard web server logs (IP addresses, timestamps, pages visited) as part of their hosting service. See [Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy) for details.

### Orchesis Software

Orchesis is self-hosted software that runs on your own infrastructure. We do not receive, collect, or have access to any data processed by your Orchesis instance. See our [PRIVACY.md](https://github.com/poushwell/orchesis/blob/main/PRIVACY.md) for details on how Orchesis handles data locally.

### GitHub

Our source code is hosted on GitHub. If you interact with our GitHub repository (starring, forking, opening issues, submitting PRs), GitHub collects data according to their privacy policy. We can see your GitHub username and public profile information.

### Email

If you email us at security@orchesis.io, we will have your email address and message content. We use this only to respond to your inquiry and will not share it with third parties.

## Your Rights

Under GDPR (if you are an EU resident) and similar laws:

- **Access:** You can ask what data we have about you (likely none beyond any emails you've sent us)
- **Deletion:** You can ask us to delete any data we have about you
- **Portability:** You can request a copy of your data in a standard format

To exercise these rights, email: security@orchesis.io

## Changes

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
